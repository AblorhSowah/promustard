About Us

Promustard Micro-Credit is an impact investing fund that focuses on helping women-led social enterprises (SEs) and micro small and medium enterprises (MSMEs) in Africa.

We are an impact finance firm. We create innovative finance mechanisms and use impact investing to scale financing solutions. Throughout our work, we serve the dual objective of impact & profit.

women-led SEs

MSME

PROMUSTARD MICROCREDIT AFRICA

Over the past years, Promustard Micro-Credit has pioneered a set of outcomes-based financing strategies that relies on transparency to directly link resources to impact the livelihoods of women-led SEs and MSMEs in Africa. We have supported SEs and MSMEs in Ghana for the past years with a credit facility of a considerably low interest rate.

Promustard Micro-Credit uses innovative finance, fund management and impact investing to scale projects with high societal and environmental impact, while offering attractive returns to our investors.

WHY WOMEN

MSMEs have played and continue to play a critical role in Africa’s socio-economic development by producing critical goods and services, creating jobs, and helping to reduce poverty and promote economic growth.

It is estimated that MSMEs represent about 90 per cent of Africa’s private sector and contribute about 85 percent of annual GDP. In terms of employment, MSMEs account for an estimated 85 percent of manufacturing jobs in the country.

The MSME sector has also provided women and the youth opportunities to harness their economic potential, and it is estimated by the World Bank that about a third of all MSMEs in Africa are owned by women.

Globally, impact investing has emerged as a strong asset class and a $502billion market. The Global Impact Investing Network 2019 Annual Impact Investor Survey showed that impact investments met or exceeded the expectations of fund managers for financial return (91%) and impact (98%).

Our Impact Investment models are designed for SEs and MSMEs to systematically leverage the capital to deliver measurable, innovative and sustainable impact for these enterprises in africa.

We envision operating within the broader Impact Investment space, a growing global movement that represents $790 billion in assets under management. Since Impact Investment is aimed at generating both positive financial return and measurable social and environmental impact, the tools deployed are unique in financial returns and dependent on impact, which is based on measurable outcomes rather than outputs.

Promustard Impact partners with Impact Investing Ghana, Global Foundations, Other Corporate Players or service providers, and some financial institutions to apply innovative financing strategies across a range of areas, including Women in MSMEs in Africa.

We provide two distinctive models on our credit facility. These include debt and grant loans. Debt loans are loans repayable on the scheduled date of a stated amount. While our grant loans are accessible freely without returns.

Promustard Impact is a financial lending house that expands access to credit facilities, and other financial services for underserved communities, including poor households and entrepreneurs in the Micro Small and Medium Enterprises space. Our Innovative Financing aims to support solutions created by women led- Social Enterprises and Micro Small and Medium Enterprises across Africa who are working on some transformational/ innovative projects.

Advisory Services

We support indigenous businesses in the private sector by providing business development services to equip them to use best practices in managing their businesses.

Our team of trainers come with decades of experience in financial accounting, unit economics, and investment portfolio management to support MSMEs in the areas of managing and assisting them with any financial challenges they face.

Most importantly, our team aims to put appropriate measures in place to help the MSMEs minimize any financial risks that they are likely to be confronted with.
